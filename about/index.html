About Us

Little Bitta is a small-batch, baked goods company based in Minnesota. We believe everyone deserves a little treat(or several!) throughout the day. Our mission is to make our community a little sweeter by providing treat that taste just like home. From the time you wake up, to right before bed, we have a Little Bitta something just for you.
